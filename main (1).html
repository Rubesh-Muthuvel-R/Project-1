Achievement


Our CSE Association has been successfully running over three decades crossing a silver jubilee. Every year we conduct events that invoke the technical and creative skills of every single individual and with the constant support of all the students. The main events of our CSE Associations are Project Expo, Technical Quiz, and combination of Lectures Series, Debate, and Photography, technical competitions, hands on workshops, exhibitions and demos of the latest innovations in technology. It comprises a diverse array of highly competitive technical and managerial events which attract our students as well as the best brains of the leading engineering institutes.
